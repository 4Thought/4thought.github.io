A FinTech Company With a Purpose

We Empower Responsible Innovation in Financial Markets

At 4Thought Technologies, our mission is to empower responsible innovation in financial markets through groundbreaking tools to increase returns and manage investment risk.

We use the power of no-code technology to democratize the benefits of algorithm trading and automated risk controls for all market participants. As a result, 4Thought also helps increase & diversify the pool of fund managers and traders that can afford the technology infrastructure required to build competitive investment firms.

About Our Name

In Greek mythology, Prometheus (whose name means “forethought”) champions humankind by providing the fire of the gods. At 4Thought Technologies, we similarly champion investors by providing newly accessible tools to level the playing field for them to succeed. Hence our name.
