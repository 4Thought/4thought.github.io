4Thought Labs

4Thought Labs pursues opportunities to license our KRONOS™ infrastructure to select enterprises and “partner” to build new products that run on top of it.

Initial Involvements

The National Science Foundation (NSF) supports the research & development of deep technologies through its competitive SBIR1 grant program and other means.


The NSF has awarded 4Thought a SBIR grant of $256K for the six-month Phase 1 build of our Smart Contracts interface. The potential grant for Phase 2 is $1 million.

Founded in 2021, the Center for Research Toward Advancing Financial Technologies (CRAFT) is the first NSF-backed research center dedicated to Fintech.


4Thought is the only startup in CRAFT and is exploring projects with fellow members that include Stevens2 & RPI2, BofA, Goldman Sachs, IBM, Vanguard and others.

1. SBIR stands for Small Business Innovation Research. 2. The Stevens Institute of Technology (Stevens) and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) are the founding members of CRAFT.
