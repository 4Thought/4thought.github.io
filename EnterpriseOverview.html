Enterprise Product Overview

4Thought Technologies empowers hedge funds to compete with institutional algorithmic traders and quant shops. No coding required.

4Thought brings advanced algo-trading and automation software that gives everyone, from business users and traders to advanced quantitative developers, the right canvas to build great trading rules—and the right governance tools to deploy and manage them all.

By removing the barrier of years of training as a software developer, 4Thought turns everyday portfolio managers with no programming background into “Citizen Algorithmic Traders” for tasks ranging from rebalancing portfolios to creating and executing complex, rules-based trading strategies. 4Thought's integrated backtesting application furthermore enables customers to pre-test their algorithms and trading theses based on historical data via a similar user-friendly interface.

4Thoughts platform works across asset classes including equities, cryptocurrencies, foreign exchange and derivatives. The deep technology developed by 4Thought is so powerful, it was even chosen for a high frequency trading project sponsored by the United States National Science Foundation.
